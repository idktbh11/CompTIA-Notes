PAN(Personal Area Network) - A small network for connecting devices around one person
LAN(Local Area Network) - A network that connects devices in a small area.
WLAN(Wireless LAN) - LAN that uses Wi-Fi instead of cables to connect devices. [made when a LAN includes an access point]
VLAN(Virtual Local Area Network) - A network on a LAN that are grouped together to act like they're on the same network.
SAN(Storage Area Network) - A network that delivers storage over a network.
WAN(Wide Area Network) - Connects devices over large distances.
MAN(Metropolitan Area Network) - A network that connects computers and devices across a large area, like a city. Bigger than a LAN but smaller than the Internet.

NFC(Near Field Communication) - A technology that allows devices to communicate wirelessly over short distances.
